Hi, I'm Violet DeMarco. I'm a software engineer/game developer from Massachusetts.

I have extensive experience with the Unity game engine, C#, C++, Python, and have a general passion for games and computers. Check out some of my work in the projects section of this site, or at my Github page.

I studied game development at Southern New Hampshire University, and graduated in April 2022. I'm passionate about games, virtual reality, PC hardware, and open-source software.

I was a part of SNHU's Inkwell Game Studio, and I've worked on several projects for them, such as an interactive archaeology simulation, a JRPG game, a small fishing game prototype, and a 3D platformer game for the Bank of New Hampshire Pavilion. I've been working at Northern Light Productions since 2022 as an interactive developer. I've been involved in 6 projects in some capacity (4 of which working on several interactives), leading the development on four of them. In my free time I like to play games, tinker with world creation in VRChat, make bad songs, and hang out with my weird cat and dog.
