NLP: Lowell "My Experience Station"

A video player interactive developed for Lowell National Historical Park.

I developed all five interactives and the content management system (CMS) for the project. The "My Experience Station" is a standard video player interactive that plays 9 curated videos submitted for "permanent" display (not totally accurate since they can be updated in the CMS), and a page that displays a random list of 9 visitor videos submitted from the Story Booth interactive. There are three "My Experience" stations, each themed around a question being answered by or about the video subjects.
