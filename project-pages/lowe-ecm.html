NLP: Lowell "Experience Culture Through Music"

A video player interactive developed for Lowell National Historical Park.

I developed all five interactives and the content management system (CMS) for the project. The "Experience Culture Through Music" interactive is a video player interactive centered around viewing performances of four musical groups, and learning about their history through small "fun fact" windows you can open at the bottom of the screen.
