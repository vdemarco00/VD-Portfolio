Race to the Stage

A time trial 3D platforming game developed for the Bank of New Hampshire Pavilion.

The Bank of New Hampshire Pavilion commissioned SNHU's Inkwell Game Studio to create a competitive game themed around the pavilion for guests at the pavilion to playing during some of their events.

We created a 3D platformer game where players will individually race through a course themed around the pavilion, and will all compete for the fastest time to the finish line. The game is played in a web browser and communicates with a leaderboard server, so players and submit their scores and compare theirs to other player scores.
